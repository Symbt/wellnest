🌿 Getting Started with WellNest

Welcome to Your Wellness Journey!

WellNest is your complete offline wellness tracker. All your data stays private on your device.
📊 Dashboard

Your home base showing today's overview, medications to take, upcoming appointments, and pending tasks. Click any card to navigate to that section.

⚖️ Weight Tracking

Log your weight and see trends with ↑ ↓ = indicators comparing to your previous entry.

💊 Medications

Track medications with start/end dates. Daily checklist shows what to take today. Mark as taken to track adherence.

📅 Appointments

Log doctor visits, tests, and appointments. Sync directly to Google Calendar with one click.

✅ Tasks

Manage wellness and life tasks with priorities, due dates, and categories.

💾 Backup Your Data

Go to Settings to download backups (JSON file or backup code) or email them to yourself. Use these to restore on other devices.

🎨 Themes

Choose from 5 beautiful themes in Settings: Default, Dark, Ocean, Sunset, or Forest.

💡 Pro Tip: Start by logging your weight and setting up your medications. The dashboard will guide you from there!
